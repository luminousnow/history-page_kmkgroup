History

2004

The beginning of the journey. Year of establishment of the group of companies "KMK Group".
From the first day of work, we strive to implement the most daring and new ideas on the construction markets of Ukraine and the world, creating optimal conditions for our consumers, taking into account the modern rhythm human and environmental protection.

2005

Official representatives of sewage treatment plants in the Czech Republic produced in Ukraine.
During the year of successful cooperation, a decision was made to expand and the start of own production of biological sewage treatment stations waters under the Topas Ukraine brand with an emphasis on global innovations and trends

2006

One step closer to protecting the environment.
The own factory for the production of a local sewage treatment plant was opened Topas Automatic. The company Topas Ukraine is successful has proven itself, both in the Ukrainian and international markets.

2009

Official representatives and importers of Greek waterproofing producer of TM Alchimica on the territory of Ukraine.
In a short period of time, Hyperdesmo waterproofing from Alchimica gained the trust of consumers throughout Ukraine.

2013

Official representatives of Belgian environmentally friendly plastics sandwich Paneltim panels on the territory of Ukraine.
C 2013 continued successful cooperation, distribution and proper production of products using this material.

2014

The Group includes a new line of activity.
Representation of chipboards for all types of decoration and Quick Deck construction works. This one is unique in its kind a product that has no analogues in the territory of Ukraine.

2019

Establishment of own brand of waterproofing - RinoFlex.
RinoFlex products have incorporated many years of Greek experience professional technologies of world-famous Alchimica waterproofing and domestic developments in the field of waterproofing production materials taking into account the natural climate of Ukraine.

2020

The production of new products under the PlastBuild TM has been launched.
Cellars, fountains, boats, bar counters and other products. Modern polypropylene products are the most reliable, durable, safe and durable

2021

Complete rebranding. We clearly see the result of activity and future direction. The logo became a representation of our slogan: Build, Rethink, Recycle. Cooperation with companies continues international level, and the equipment of its own production won trust of customers in all corners of Ukraine, the CIS countries, the Baltic States, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan and Equatorial Guinea.

2022

The war makes adjustments to the company's work. It stops cooperation with the company for the production of TM Quick products Deck.
Rebooting. We understand that we can help ours activity to the Ukrainian population. Production of shelters has begun under the Shelter Paneltim brand, made of Belgian PanelTim plastic.

"KMK Group" today is a dynamically developing brand that started its activity in 2004 and during that time successfully has proven itself on the Ukrainian and international markets.
Over the past years, KMK Group has transformed from a small enterprise into a large structure, the partners of which are leading European ones producers.
The production base of KMK Group makes it possible to reach Ukrainian market and regions far beyond.
